Frequently Asked Questions:

1. How to Register

In order to register, you must click register from the home page and fill out the required information. Click Register to make your account.

2. How to file a report

Click 'report a problem' from the home page and enter all the required information to file a problem.

3. How to login

In order to login, you must be a registered user. Click login from the home page and login using your username and password.

4. How to contact us

Click on the 'contact us' tab from the home page, which will redirect you to our contact page.

5. What if I forgot my password

If you forgot your password, you will be asked a security question and you will be emailed a link to reset your password.

6. Why can't I login?

If you have enetered the incorrect password 3 times, then you login has been banned for an hour for security purposes. Please try again in an hour or contact our technical services department.
